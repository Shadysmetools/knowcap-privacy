Knowcap Browser Extension

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Introduction

Knowcap ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use the Knowcap Chrome Extension.

By using our extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Recording Data

  • Meeting Recordings: When you initiate a recording, we capture audio and video from your screen or browser tab as explicitly requested by you.
  • Meeting Metadata: We collect meeting URLs, titles, and timestamps to organize your recordings.

Account Information

  • Authentication Data: Email address and authentication tokens when you sign in to your Knowcap account.
  • User Preferences: Your extension settings stored locally in your browser.

Technical Data

  • Display Information: Screen dimensions used solely to position UI elements.
  • Tab Information: Current tab URL to detect Google Meet sessions (only accessed when you interact with the extension).

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Record and transcribe your meetings as requested
  • Upload recordings to your Knowcap workspace
  • Sync your settings across devices
  • Provide recording status notifications
  • Improve extension functionality and user experience

Data Storage & Security

Data Type Storage
Extension Settings Local (Chrome Storage)
Authentication Tokens Local (Encrypted)
Meeting Recordings Cloud (AWS S3)
Account Data Cloud (Supabase)

All data transmissions use HTTPS/TLS encryption. Cloud storage is encrypted at rest.

Third-Party Services

Our extension integrates with trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase: Secure database and authentication
  • AWS: Secure cloud storage for recordings
  • Recall.ai: Enhanced meeting bot functionality

These services maintain their own privacy policies and meet our security standards.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share data only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your recorded data through your Knowcap dashboard
  • Delete your recordings at any time
  • Export your data
  • Close your account and have all data deleted

Data Retention

  • Recordings are retained in your account until you delete them
  • Deleted recordings are permanently removed within 30 days
  • Account deletion removes all associated data

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the extension or via email.
